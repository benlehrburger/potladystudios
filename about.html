Sharyn Kohen

I’m a retired financial services consulting executive with a growing passion for ceramics. After years spent helping clients solve complex problems, I realized the part of consulting I loved most was the creative challenge of working through something new and using familiar tools in unfamiliar ways.

My work draws inspiration from two long-standing passions: gardening, especially container gardening, and the thrill of finding vintage and industrial objects that carry texture, history, and character.

Pot Lady Studios is a place to share my work, my process, and the inspiration I find in the garden, in old objects, and in the joy of a new challenge each day.
